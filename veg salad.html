Vegetable Salad

Benefits of Vegetable

Aside from their natural good taste and crunchy texture alongside wonderful colors and fragrances,eating a large serving of fresh, raw vegetables each day can have significant health benefits

Steps to make Vegetable Salad

  • Take a large and clear glass bowl
  • Try adding blanched,lightly streamed or roasted veggies like broccoli,french beans,corn etc...
  • Add lentils,beans or chickpeas or sprouts for more protien
  • Grate some of the vegetables for a different texture
  • Add marinated veggies like Artichoke hearts for tang,other veggies like raddish
  • Swap in your own favourite combination of nuts and seads
  • Use fresh coriander otherwise instead of parsley.
This easy vegetable salad is packed with a variety of summer produce with all the texture and colors one could ever want
